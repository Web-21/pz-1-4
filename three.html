Сафарі-тур "Гадяцький"

Gadyach tour is an exciting trip to the picturesque city of Gadyach, in the Poltava region, rich in history and cultural heritage. Visitors can get acquainted with architectural monuments, ancient churches, historical museums and picturesque landscapes around the city. Gadyach is known as the center of cultural and political life during the Hetmanship, as well as the birthplace of prominent figures of Ukrainian history. The tour offers an opportunity to feel the spirit of ancient traditions and immerse yourself in the atmosphere of ancient Ukraine.
