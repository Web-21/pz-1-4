Тур вихідного дня "Гостинність Чорнобаївки

The mystery of the Snake Island, hidden in the Black Sea, has troubled the imagination of people for centuries. Locals believed that ancient snakes endowed with magical powers once lived on this small piece of land. At night, as the old legends said, the island came to life: strange creatures came out of the dungeons, and the sky above it turned black and red.

Відгуки

Snake Island is a real mystery of nature. It feels like you are at the edge of the world, and the atmosphere is filled with mystery. Heard from local fishermen interesting legends about treasures and strange phenomena that supposedly happen here. It is amazing that such a small island has such a rich history.

Zmiiny is not just a picturesque place, but a real historical treasure. Legends about ancient civilizations and mysterious events add even more appeal to it. During the trip, I felt the special energy of this island, and its isolation exudes ancient magic. An ideal place for those who love mysterious places with deep roots.
