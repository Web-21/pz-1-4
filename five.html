Квест-тур "Київ за 3 дні"

Tour "Kyiv in 3 days" is a rich journey through the capital of Ukraine, which will allow you to see the most famous historical and cultural sights of the city. The program of the tour includes visits to St. Sophia Cathedral, Kyiv-Pechersk Lavra, Independence Square, Andriyivsky Uzvoz and other key places. Walks along the Dnipro River, modern museums and gastronomic stops will complement the impression of Kyiv. The tour is designed to experience the spirit and beauty of the city as much as possible in a short time.
