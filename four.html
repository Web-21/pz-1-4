Майстер-клас “Ромський”

Master class "Roma" is an exciting event dedicated to Roma culture and traditions. Participants will be able to get acquainted with the art of Roma dances, music and crafts, as well as learn about the rich cultural heritage of the Roma people. The master class offers an opportunity not only to learn the basic dance movements, but also to immerse yourself in the bright world of Roma culture through interactive classes and live performances.
